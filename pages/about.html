About the Bachelor of Information Technology

The Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) programme is designed to equip students with practical skills in software development, networking, data systems, and cybersecurity. This multi-disciplinary programme prepares graduates to solve real-world challenges using modern digital tools.

Our Mission

To produce highly skilled IT professionals with the competence to innovate, build, manage and secure digital systems used in business, government, and society.

Why Choose BIT?

  • Hands‑on practical labs & industry projects
  • Modern curriculum aligned with global IT standards
  • Internship placement support
  • Experienced lecturers with industry backgrounds
  • Opportunities for innovation, competitions, and tech clubs
