Admissions

Join the Bachelor of Information Technology program and build your future in tech.

Entry Requirements

  • A high school diploma or equivalent.
  • Pass grades in Mathematics and English.
  • Basic computer literacy is an added advantage.
  • Completion of the national or institutional entry exam.

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Upload required documents (grades, ID, passport photo).
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Wait for admission confirmation via email or portal.

Important Dates

  • Applications Open: January
  • Screening: March
  • Final Selection: April
  • Registration: June
